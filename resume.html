North Shore Culture Quest

React-Native, NextJS CMS /w PostgreSQL

* Launched Spring 2025, available now on App Stores

In partnership with North Van Arts, I designed and developed this app for local heritage & tourism purposes. Users can explore the North Shore on foot and test their knowledge of what they learn with engaging trivia game functionality. Bear illustrations were provided by a local artist. The app is made with React-Native utilizing Zustand & tanstack-query for state management.

Triviography

React-Native, Custom Socket.io Real-Time Backend, NextJS CMS /w PostgreSQL

* Currently in Beta Testing, demo upon request. Current web-link is for old project by previous developers

I designed and developed Triviography for White Mountain Adventures in Banff, Alberta from Summer 2025. It is a location-based trivia game where users can interact with each other via messaging and location-sharing in real-time while competing to answer location based multi-media trivia questions in a specific area. Real-time communication is handled by a custom Socket.io-based backend coupled with Jotai-based state-management in-app.

Casca Footwear

React-Native, Shopify API Integration, 3D Printing Service Integration

* Casca is now out of business and the app is no longer live.

I designed and developed the Casca Footwear app for their customers to be able to produce a 3D model of their foot by taking 3 precisely oriented images. Combined with their purchase data and personal preferences this data was then used to 3D print-insoles at an in-house 3D printing lab. The app integrated with Shopify as well as 3D printing software & hardware

CEO.ca

Custom NodeJS Web, Redis/Socket.io/ZMQ Real-Time Services

CEO.ca is a high-performance real-time stock market data processing service and chat-based community for Canadian financial markets. Along with software engineer Murat Ayfer, we built CEO.ca from the ground up with a Node.js, Redis, PostgreSQL, Socket.io, and ZeroMQ. Years later it is still serving thousands of daily free & paid users to keep them informed on their investments.
