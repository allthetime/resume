On This Spot

CTO, Developer, Architect

App, Web, Data, Ops, Management

On This Spot is a historical education and walking tour app based on the exact location of old photos. Users can visit these spots and compare and contrast the past and the present through interactive photography and high quality written history.

The android and ios apps are made with react-native and make heavy use of the mapbox library. Custom GLSL is used to create an image editor that allows users to make their own then & now photos.

The website is a mixure of react components and server-rendered templates that is currently being re-written in nextjs to close the static/dynamic divide.

The backend includes a collection of node-express apis, a postgres database, and a small image resizing and caching service written in rust.

All code written by me and co-op students under my mentorship and direction.
